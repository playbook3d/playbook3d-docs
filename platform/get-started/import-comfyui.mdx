---
title: "Import ComfyUI Workflow"
---

You can import ComfyUI workflows (.json) into Playbook so long as it is comprised of our whitelisted list of nodes. When you import a ComfyUI workflow, it is converted to a Playbook workflow and any further changes made to the original file in ComfyUI will not be reflected in Playbook.

<Info>
  Supports ComfyUI version: **v0.0.30**
</Info>

## Tips for configuring your ComfyUI workflow for import

1. Comment out all preview or output nodes that you do not want outputted
2. Do not use `GET` or `SET` nodes
3. Ensure all necessary connections are made
